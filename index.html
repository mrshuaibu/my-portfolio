About me

My name is Oluwajuwon Michael Shuaibu from Port Harcourt, Rvers State, Nigeria. Growing up, many aspired to migrate to countries like the United States and Canada for better opportunities. I have always embraced learning and approached life with an open mind.

My passion for technology began in childhood, where I enjoyed taking apart devices and reassembling them. This curiosity about how we evolved from the Stone Age to modern technology motivated me to pursue a deeper understanding of software development.

With a solid education, I realized that formal training was essential for mastering technology. I initially studied Computer Science at the University of Manitoba before transferring to MITT for a more hands-on approach in software development.
